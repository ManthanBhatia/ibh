And lastly, are we future ready?

As stated by UNEP, food wastage contributes 8-10% of greenhouse gas emissions. We endeavour to contribute in a small but significant way in the war against climate change.
Also, we aspire to make "Organic Farming" the way- of-life and reduce the dependence on chemicals and fertilizers.
We try to engage stakeholders in such a manner that the well being of this planet becomes everyone's business.
